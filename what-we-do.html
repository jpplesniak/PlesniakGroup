What We Do

Strategic Acquisition & Growth Partnership

We Acquire Valuable Cash-Generating Businesses

Plesniak Group is dedicated to identifying, acquiring, and growing small businesses that demonstrate strong fundamentals and sustainable cash generation. We're looking to acquire businesses from owners who are ready for their next chapter—whether that's retirement, a new venture, or simply moving on.

Our Goal

Our goal is simple: create a win-win situation where both parties walk away satisfied. We believe in fair valuations based on real numbers, transparent negotiations, and a smooth transition process. Our job is to offer you a fair deal, handle the process professionally, and ensure your business continues to thrive under new ownership.

Our Acquisition Process

1

Initial Conversation

We start with a confidential discussion about your business, its operations, and your goals for transition or sale.

2

Due Diligence

We conduct thorough analysis of financials, operations, and growth potential while respecting the confidentiality of your business.

3

Structuring the Deal

We work collaboratively to create a transaction structure that honors what you've built and provides a fair outcome.

4

Growth & Preservation

Post-acquisition, we focus on preserving what works while strategically building what's next for continued success.

What We're Looking For

💰 Cash Flow Positive

Businesses generating consistent, positive cash flow with clear financial fundamentals.

📊 Proven Track Record

Established operations with a history of serving customers and delivering value.

🌱 Growth Potential

Opportunities for expansion, optimization, or strategic enhancement.

🏢 Small to Mid-Size

Focused on businesses where we can make a meaningful impact and add value.

🎯 Strong Foundation

Solid operational systems, customer relationships, and business processes in place.

🤝 Owner Ready

Business owners considering transition, retirement, or pursuing new opportunities.
