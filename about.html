About Plesniak Group

Preserving What Works. Building What's Next.

Our Mission

At Plesniak Group, we believe that great businesses deserve to continue thriving. Our mission is to identify and acquire cash-generating businesses with strong fundamentals, then preserve what makes them successful while strategically building toward their next chapter of growth.

Meet the Founder

JonPaul (JP) Plesniak

Founder & Principal

Born in 1999 and raised in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, JP has always had a passion for building and creating value. He attended the University of Pittsburgh, where he earned a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering, along with a Certificate in Innovation & Product Development and minors in Bioengineering and Chemistry.

After graduating in 2022, JP began his career as a Product Manager at Dick's Sporting Goods, where he leads technology initiatives and manages complex projects with multi-million dollar impacts. His analytical mindset, coupled with hands-on engineering experience, gives him a unique perspective on business operations, systems optimization, and value creation.

JP founded Plesniak Group with a vision to apply his product development expertise and financial acumen to acquiring and growing established businesses. As a passionate investor, he brings a data-driven, customer-focused approach to every opportunity.
